Description

Welcome to this stunning 1913 brick carriage house in Denny Blaine, where luxury style living and nature come together to create the ultimate lifestyle! Once part of the original Denny Blaine Estate, it was meticulously remodeled in 1982 by renowned architect George Suyama. This home is truly unique, with a wall of windows letting abundant natural light through to the main living spaces. Vaulted skylights shower the home in light, and create an awe-inspiring 2-story atrium with a highly desirable open floor plan. A gas fireplace keeps things warm as you step out to the spacious rooftop patio, which overlooks the lake. Privacy abounds, inside the home and out. Replete w/ a 2-car garage w/ EV charging, and a tranquil garden with lake views.
