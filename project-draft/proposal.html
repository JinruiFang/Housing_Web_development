Problem Description

Purchasing a home is a significant life decision. People will spend most of their time at home, perhaps starting a family and making lovely memories. For many people, investing in real estate is the most huge financial decision they will ever make. As a result, before purchasing a home, people should conduct some research on their desired properties, so the acquisition of information is particularly important for prospective buyers. However, there is often too much information behind a real estate property, and it can be time consuming for buyers to fully integrate every information, and they may also forget to investigate certain key aspects.

The problem could be troubling for some new home buyers. There are some real estate apps on the market such as Zillow that solve this problem by integrating property information, construction information, HOA information, and financial information and show them via their apps. They make it easier and faster for users to obtain information about properties. However Zillow also has some defects. For example, potential buyers may be interested in a property's previous sales record, which Zillow does not provide. In fact, it is not difficult to obtain sales records because they are public records. Therefore, we can make a real estate information sharing website, including but not limited to sales records.

We are fully aware of the importance of openness and symmetry of information for the acquisition of property by our clients. In addition to the problems mentioned earlier: the lack of prior sales record may make zillow unable to meet the needs of some people's inquiries about specific listings; there are many other issues that we should address. For example, whether the information on the app is consistent with the information on the real estate company's website; whether the app can be updated to reflect any changes in information, etc.

From a more macro perspective, providing complete information and convenience to customers is not only beneficial for both buyers and sellers, but also for optimizing the spatial layout of the city, especially for implementing public policies such as affordable housing. Firstly, the openness of information shortens the time customers spend hesitating in choosing properties and thus reduces housing market frictions. For one thing, the reduced friction decreases the number of vacant housing, and increases property utilization rates. For another, it gives government agencies more response time on land use planning when facing changing patterns.

So our core purpose is to provide users with a platform that is as detailed as possible, and to provide customers with as many listing options as possible while ensuring that the information is accurate, so that they can make accurate decisions to meet their needs. At the same time, it allows public policies to benefit a wider audience and contribute to social equity.

Proposed Application Description

The potential users of this webpage would be first of all some real estate traders. Buyers can use this website to compare the information of houses with each other and then help them to make a better judgment and buy a more satisfactory house. People who are selling their homes can also use this site to get information about the prices of other homes and other information to gauge what they want to sell. For people who are new to Seattle and are thinking of buying a home, this website can help them to quickly learn about Seattle real estate first to help them choose a home.

These users, who can see some exterior and also interior photos of a house on this website, can get a preliminary idea of the basic information of the house. They can also access specific information about the house, such as geographic information and price. We will also put on the website how to buy the house, so that they can contact the seller when they want to buy. This can be more efficient and convenient. Also, we will put some area information such as security, education, health care, etc. to help users to choose the area to buy a house.

Our users will first see the main page that introduces the Top 5 most viewed Buildings around them. They can find a specific building that they like by using the search bar. There will also be a filter that allows the users to change their budget, location, building types, etc. Every building has its own page that shows detailed information including the floor plan, the contact information of the owner, and also price range.

This app will help our target users to find useful information about the buildings around them. Our app can generate a list of popular buildings, which can encourage the surrounding competitiveness. The overall quality of the buildings will be increased among the competition.
